Molly Bonus

  • Matching Jack in reputation → +2 joy each time a desire enters your reputation in that suit.
  • Matching Queen → +3 joy. Both Jack & Queen → +5 joy.
  • ⚠ Add mollies to reputation before desires (so new desires still trigger the bonus).

Foiling Threats & Pride Rule

When a threat is selected (scored) at a festivity:

  1. Add threat to safe pile.
  2. That player (or all, if community scored) adds all matching reputation cards → safe pile.
  3. Pride Rule (optional, all-or-nothing): take 1 matching cube per card you added to the safe pile.

End of the Week (4 phases)

1
Reveal Threats. Each player reveals all threats in hand → gossip pile + exposed. Gain 1 draw token if you revealed ≥1 threat.
2
Society Investigates.
  • Shuffle gossip; remove ⌊total ÷ 3⌋ to safe pile (round down); reveal rest.
  • 💡 Domino item: play here, before revealing, to ignore your reputation during raids.
  • Each threat → 1 evidence per matching desire/molly → place on that house.
  • ≥7 evidence on an open house → RAIDED: place markers; remove 7 evidence; most rep → major indictment; 2nd most → minor indictment.
  • Check game end (see below).
3
Encounter the Society. Players who gained indictments place 1 encounter token face-down on nearest open house (clockwise tie-break). Skip if 0–1 tokens.
4
Cleanup.
  1. Shuffle all vice cards not in hands/reputations/market → new vice deck.
  2. Refill market; add 1 card face-down to gossip.
  3. Players spend draw tokens (draw 1 card per token).
  4. Discard shop items; deal new items to each shop.
  5. Advance week marker; resume with candelabra holder.

Game Endings

💀 Community Infiltration

All 4 houses raided, or a raided house reaches ≥7 evidence.

  • Reveal all encounter tokens.
  • Non-informers & informers at open houses → hanged.
  • Informers at raided house → not hanged; joy = 0.
  • Informer score: +2 VP/evidence at house; +2 VP/matching rep of others; −1 VP/own reputation (all suits). Highest positive score wins; ties/no positive = all lose.

🎉 Community Survival

Community joy reaches threshold before game otherwise ends.

  • Reveal encounter tokens & indictments.
  • Successful informers (token at raided house): discard all indictments.
  • Failed informers (token at open house): +1 major indictment.
  • +2 joy per loyal token at an open house.
  • Indictment trials: roll d10 per card; lose that joy. Noose = hanged.
  • Highest unhanged joy wins; ties share victory.

💨 Community Atrophy

Fifth week ends with no Infiltration or Survival.

All players lose.
